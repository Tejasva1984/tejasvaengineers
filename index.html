About Us

Tejasva Engineers is a PESO-recognized third-party inspection agency, certified as a “Competent Person” under SMPV(U) Rules, 2016 (Letter No. CPS111515 dated 05.06.2025). We specialize in engineering design, inspection, certification, and licensing services for cryogenic, non-cryogenic, and hydrocarbon pressure vessels.

Our Services

Design & Engineering

  • Layout Plans / Site Plans / 100m Radius Key Plans
  • Mounting Drawings for Cryogenic & Non-Cryogenic Vessels
  • Civil Foundation Drawings
  • Engineering Drawings for Cryogenic (LOX, LIN, LAR), Non-Cryogenic (CO₂, Chlorine, Ammonia), and Hydrocarbon (LPG, Propane) Vessels

Third-Party Inspection & Certification

  • SRV Testing (Rule 18)
  • Hydrostatic Testing (Rule 19)
  • Safety Certification of Storage Installations (Rule 33)
  • Safety Certification of Transport Tankers (Rule 43)

Licensing Services (PESO Norms)

  • License / Renewal / Amendment (Form LS1 & LS2)
  • Prior Approval to Final PESO Endorsement

Installation, Erection & Maintenance

  • Cryogenic Tank Installation at Hospitals, Steel Plants, Pharma Units, Gas Industries
  • Gas Cylinder Filling Station Setup & Certification

Maintenance Services

  • Preventive Maintenance of Cryogenic & Non-Cryogenic Vessels
  • Maintenance of Hydrocarbon Storage Vessels

Cylinder Testing Station

Import Vessel Approval

  • Facilitation of PESO Approval for Imported Pressure Vessels
